Senior Machine Learning Engineer

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our AI platform team. You will design, build, and maintain production ML systems that power our core product features. This is a hands-on engineering role with significant technical scope.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement scalable ML pipelines and model serving infrastructure
- Collaborate with data scientists to productionize models using MLOps best practices
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines for ML model deployment
- Optimize model performance for latency, throughput, and cost
- Lead technical design reviews and mentor junior team members
- Work with platform teams to build internal ML tooling and frameworks

Required Qualifications:
- 3+ years experience in production Machine Learning Engineering
- Strong proficiency in Python and ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with MLflow, Kubeflow, or similar MLOps tools
- Proficiency in SQL and distributed data processing (Spark)
- Strong understanding of software engineering best practices (Git, CI/CD)

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with model monitoring and feature stores
- Knowledge of NLP, computer vision, or recommendation systems
- Contributions to open-source projects
- M.S. or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field

We Offer:
- Competitive salary and equity
- Remote-friendly work environment
- Learning & development budget
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
